Teaching

In Trimester 1 of 2025, I was afforded the opportunity to work alongside Deakin University as an Associate Teaching Fellow for SIT111: Computer Systems. Part of my role includes guiding students, hosting HelpHub sessions, and giving actionable feedback to tasks. Areas covered in this unit include:

  • Computer Architecture: boolean algebra, logic gates, and binary number representation, memory, file systems
  • Operating Systems: virtual machines, shell scripting, and containerisation via Docker
  • Hardware: sensors, actuators, resistors, and Arduino programming

During my time as an Associate Teaching Fellow, I gained experience in the areas of study that students frequently struggled with. Due to this, I created a series of short videos to help simplify the complex, challenging concepts explored in this subject.
