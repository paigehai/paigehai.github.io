In Trimester 1 of 2025, I was afforded the opportunity to work alongside Deakin University as an Associate Teaching Fellow for SIT111: Computer Systems. Part of my teaching includes guiding students, hosting HelpHub sessions, and giving actionable feedback to tasks. Areas covered in this unit include:

<ul>
  <li><strong>Computer Architecture:</strong> boolean algebra, logic gates, and binary number representation, memory, file systems</li>
  <li><strong>Operating Systems:</strong> virtual machines, shell scripting, and containerisation via Docker</li>
  <li><strong>Hardware:</strong> sensors, actuators, resistors, and Arduino programming</li>
</ul>

During my time as an Associate Teaching Fellow, I gained experience in the areas of study that students frequently struggled with. Due to this, I created a series of short videos to help simplify the complex, challenging concepts explored in this subject.
